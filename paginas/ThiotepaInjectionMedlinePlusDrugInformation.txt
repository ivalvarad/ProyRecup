It is also used to treat malignant effusions (a condition when fluid collects in the lungs or around the heart) that are caused by cancerous tumors. Thiotepa is in a class of medications called alkylating agents. It works by slowing or stopping the growth of cancer cells in your body.         should this medicine be                           comes as a powder to be mixed with liquid to be injected intravenously (into a vein) by a doctor or nurse in a medical facility.

Nasal desmopressin is also used to control excessive thirst and the passage of an abnormally large amount of urine that may occur after a head injury or after certain types of surgery. One brand of desmopressin nasal spray ® is used to stop bleeding in people who have certain bleeding disorders. Desmopressin is in a class of medications called hormones.

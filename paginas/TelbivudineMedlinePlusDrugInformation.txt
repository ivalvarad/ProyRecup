Telbivudine is in a class of medications called nucleoside analogues.  It works by decreasing the amount of hepatitis B virus (HBV) in the body.  Telbivudine does not cure hepatitis B and may not prevent complications of chronic hepatitis B, such as cirrhosis of the liver or liver cancer. Telbivudine does not prevent the spread of hepatitis B to other people through sexual contact, sharing needles, or contact with blood.         should this medicine be                           comes as a tablet to take by mouth.

Brentuximab vedotin injection is also used to treat systemic anaplastic large cell lymphoma (sALCL; a type of non-Hodgkin lymphoma) who did not respond to another treatment period of chemotherapy. Brentuximab vedotin injection is in a class of medications called antibody-drug conjugates.
